SDF & Flow Field Engine

How SDF Pathfinding works:

Unlike standard pathfinding (like A*) where each enemy calculates its path separately, this world utilizes an **SDF Dijkstra Flow Field Map** generated dynamically starting from the Base Temple at value 0.0.

The distance increments as we move outwards. Placing walls or towers changes the distance field, causing enemies to adjust their direction to follow the negative gradient of the field automatically.

Anti-Cheat Protocol

You cannot completely block any spawn point from reaching the Base Temple. The system validates potential paths before finalizing tower builds!
