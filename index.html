ABOUT THE SEQUEL

Ordinary Defense 2

Ordinary Defense 2 is the official sequel to Damio Richter's first agentically coded game inside of Fiver's Up IT! Now, him and Google are building our own 3D version!

Controls
  • Left Click on the grid to build a Gatling Turret (Cost: 50¢)
  • Click a Tower to Select, Upgrade or Sell it.
  • Right Click + Drag to Rotate Camera.
  • Esc Key to cancel build mode.
  • Scroll to Zoom in/out.
Anti-Cheat Protocol

You cannot completely block any spawn point from reaching the Outpost HQ. The system validates potential paths before finalizing tower builds!
